About LADOC

What even is Like a Deck of Cards?

Our Story

We noticed three big problems

Phishing and scams are prevalent everywhere;

Security tools are inconvenient and scattered;

Lack of adequate digital awareness training.



So we built Like a Deck of Cards (LADOC)

Not just another tool, but an entire toolkit. A Swiss Army Knife of sorts.

A single place where you can learn, check and act, without needing to be an expert.



What does LADOC mean?

Your Deck of Cards

Your data - credentials, emails, files

Vulnerable, unprotected and scattered

But when you know how to play your hand, you're in control.



Our Deck of Cards

Our tools: lightweight, pocket-sized, and a full suit

(literally, since it's available even on mobile)

Your security, simplified Like a Deck of Cards



What we Bring to the Table

Multi-purpose Web App

Perfect for quick access anywhere, our Web App offers many tools, like:

URL & QR, email checking tools

that are *completely client-side and doesn't store your information*

As well as on-the-go security modules and quizzes to keep your instincts sharp



Fully-fledged Desktop App

Encompassing the above tools, our Desktop App also offers

Password manager, CIS benchmark checking tools,

File scanner and more

A Deck of Cards *only you can see* - we won't even know what passwords you key in!
