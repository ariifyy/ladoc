About

What even is Like a Deck of Cards?

What is LADOC?

Think Swiss army knife — Like a Deck of Cards (LADOC) is a free, growing collection of security tools that help you stay safe and smart online. The name is symbolic: your personal data is like a deck of cards — sensitive, valuable, and easily scattered. Our tools? Like a deck of cards in your hands, ready to protect.


Why LADOC?

From weak passwords to phishing links and QR scams — the digital world is full of traps. Yet, security tools remain fragmented, overly technical, or hidden behind paywalls. LADOC was built with accessibility in mind. Inspired by tools like CyberChef used in CTFs, we wanted everything in one place — free, easy, and effective. Staying safe online shouldn’t require a cybersecurity degree.


Who is LADOC for?

LADOC is designed for everyone who cares aobut online safety. Looking to do simple security checks, verify links or QR codes? Or wanting to learn more about how to be tech safe and smart? LADOC is the place for you.


Security & Privacy

LADOC is fully client-side — nothing you enter is stored or sent to a server. No passwords or credentials are ever saved, and we never log or track your links or QR codes. In fact, we can't see anything you type.
